About Us

Since we were established in 1998, we have striven to provide an experience which allows you escape from the subliminal stresses of everyday life, whilst toning up and feeling good from the inside out.

Privately owned and run by the Hutchings family, we’re able to deliver a personal spa experience that few of the big chains can match. If you are looking to join a club where you can relax in an atmosphere that is both friendly and private, then Knowle Grange may be just what you need.
